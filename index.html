Who We Are?

We come from a little town in Nebraska. We didn't want to be farmers, so Tony asked us if we want to play in a band and that's what we've been doing for over 50 years now. However, believe it or not, we still love it. We so much enjoy being on the stage, it gives us loads of energy. Of course it wouldn't be possible without you - our fans. It's really amazing that you still want to listen to our music, go to our concerts, support us in what we do. This gives us strength to keep going, and every time we feel bad and miserable, we always remind ourselves, that there are people out there, who still want us to do what we are doing. We're hoping to be there for you as long as we can!
